\section{Conclusion}

This paper summarizes the current PALM\_CGK\_BASE evidence for B6, a ResNet18 + BNNeck + ArcFace model for cross-session palmprint recognition. Under bidirectional 3-seed Tongji evaluation, B6 improves over the strong B1 ResNet18 + CE + SupCon baseline by +1.70 percentage points in Rank-1, -0.51 percentage points in EER, and +3.63 percentage points in TAR@FAR=1e-3.

The supported conclusion is scoped: BNNeck + ArcFace substantially improves cross-session Tongji palmprint verification over B1, but it is not universally superior across all datasets. On IITD within split, B6 remains competitive but does not outperform B1. The current evidence therefore supports a cross-session Tongji paper direction, with future work needed for external validation and broader robustness claims.
