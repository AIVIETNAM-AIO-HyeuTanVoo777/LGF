\section{Ablation Study}

The seed42 ablation compares B1, B4, B6, and B7 under Tongji bidirectional averaging. This isolates the effect of replacing CE + SupCon with ArcFace, adding BNNeck, and adding a light SupCon term on top of BNNeck + ArcFace.

\begin{table}[t]
\centering
\caption{Tongji seed42 bidirectional ablation.}
\label{tab:ablation}
\resizebox{\columnwidth}{!}{%
\begin{tabular}{llcccccc}
\toprule
Method & Description & Rank-1 & Rank-5 & Macro-F1 & EER & TAR@1e-2 & TAR@1e-3 \\
\midrule
B1 & ResNet18 + CE + SupCon & 93.39 & 95.47 & 92.43 & 2.30 & 96.23 & 89.75 \\
B4 & ResNet18 + ArcFace & 94.35 & 96.40 & 93.65 & 2.03 & 96.83 & 91.86 \\
B6 & ResNet18 + BNNeck + ArcFace & 96.80 & 97.88 & 96.40 & 1.14 & 98.77 & 96.53 \\
B7 & ResNet18 + BNNeck + ArcFace + light SupCon & 96.97 & 97.98 & 96.60 & 1.13 & 98.77 & 96.50 \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}%
}
\end{table}

The ablation supports three conclusions. First, ArcFace alone improves over the B1 baseline: B4 improves Rank-1 from 93.39\% to 94.35\% and TAR@FAR=1e-3 from 89.75\% to 91.86\%. Second, BNNeck + ArcFace gives the main gain: B6 reaches 96.80\% Rank-1 and 96.53\% TAR@FAR=1e-3. Third, adding light SupCon in B7 does not materially improve over B6. B7 has similar Rank-1 and EER, but its TAR@FAR=1e-3 is 96.50\%, slightly below B6's 96.53\%.

Based on this evidence, B6 is selected as the final method rather than B7.
